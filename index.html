Reach is not just followers

We help projects choose creators by influence quality, not vanity numbers.

A creator with 12,000 trusted followers can outperform a creator with 200,000 cold followers if the audience is active, on-niche, and ready to take action. Our matching logic is built around useful reach: who sees the content, who trusts the creator, and whether the creator can explain the product clearly.

01 Audience relevance

Does this creator speak to traders, builders, degens, gamers, collectors, or airdrop hunters?

02 Content strength

Can they write, explain, story-tell, record, or present the project in a way people understand?

03 Trust signals

Sorsa score, profile consistency, pinned content, audience quality, and previous campaign context.

04 Execution fit

Budget band, urgency, region, availability, video format, and whether the brief is realistic.

Quick answers

Questions projects and creators ask first.

Do you choose creators only by follower count?

No. Follower count is only one signal. We care more about audience relevance, content strength, trust, region, urgency fit, and whether the creator can explain the project in a way that moves people.

Can creators set custom pricing?

Yes. The final pricing band is $1,500+ - Custom, which is useful for larger launch campaigns, bundles, or premium creator work.

Does the X collection work automatically?

The front-end is ready for it, but live X data requires a backend/API connection. Once connected, project cards can show followers, pinned tweet, notable followers, and stronger reach-audit signals.

What does the agency do after a request arrives?

We review the brief, check creator fit, flag weak matches, confirm availability, and coordinate outreach so projects are not negotiating blindly with random accounts.
